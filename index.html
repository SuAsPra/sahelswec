Sahel Women Education Connect

Who We Are

Sahel Women Education Connect (SWEC) is a grassroots initiative dedicated to empowering conflict-affected and vulnerable women & girls of the Sahel region of Cameroon and beyond.

SWEC works to advance the educational and socio-economic opportunities of women and youth. We believe education is not just knowledge‚Äîit is a bridge to independence, leadership, and inclusion.

Through language training, leadership development, and socio-educational programs, we help women and youth connect locally, regionally, and globally, unlocking their potential to lead positive change in their communities.

Our Vision

SWEC strives to offer global volunteers venues for learning and safely practicing leadership skills. We are a unique organization that believes in absolute transparency and functions almost entirely through the efforts of its unpaid volunteers from all over the world.
