Sahel Women Education Connect

About Us

Sahel Women Education Connect (SWEC) is a community-driven initiative dedicated to transforming the lives of conflict-affected, vulnerable women and girls in the Sahel regions of Cameroon and beyond.

Our Story

The Sahel Women Education Connect (SWEC) was officially founded in May 2024. We believe that education and empowerment are powerful tools for change. Through English language training, leadership development, and socio-educational programs, SWEC equips women and youth with the skills, confidence, and opportunities they need to build brighter futures. Our mission is to create safe and inclusive spaces where women and young people can learn, grow, and lead ‚Äî breaking barriers and fostering resilience, independence, and hope for their communities.
